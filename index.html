THE THREE COALITIONS

Explore the factions, fighting philosophies, and beliefs concerning Shadow Energy.

🛡️

THE LEGION

“Eradicate the Shadow corruption at all costs.”

A stern, militaristic medieval faction that views Shadow Energy as a dangerous plague. They value physical strength, discipline, and heavy armor above all else.

Combat Specialty: Unbreakable State (Hyperarmor).

During specific attack frames, Legion warriors cannot be staggered by enemy hits, allowing them to trade blows and deal massive damage.

Weapons: Greatswords, Hammers, Flails, Axes
🐉

THE DYNASTY

“Harness the flow of energy to enrich humanity.”

A vibrant, Chinese-inspired imperial coalition representing thousands of martial arts schools. They believe Shadow Energy should be integrated into daily life and tech.

Combat Specialty: High-Hit-Count Combos.

Acrobatic, quick, and fluid. Dynasty attacks focus on rapid multi-hits that continuous stagger enemies, charging their Shadow Energy rapidly.

Weapons: Staffs, Guandao, Nunchaku, Dual Swords
👁️

THE HERALDS

“Analyze, experiment, and perfect through science.”

An enigmatic, advanced Japanese-inspired faction. They treat Shadow Energy with scientific precision, designing advanced gear, cybernetics, and nanotech.

Combat Specialty: Critical Hits & Spacing.

Precise and clinical. Heralds rely on high-reach weapons, rapid counter-attacks, and guaranteed critical strikes that knock down opponents.

Weapons: Katanas, Glaives, Sai, Kunai

SHADOW FIGHT CHRONOLOGY

Follow the historical timeline of the universe and understand the tactical balance between the factions.

Era I: The Gates of Shadows

Shadow's Mistake (SF1 & SF2)

A nameless, boastful warrior searches for a worthy opponent and opens the Gates of Shadows. Unleashed demons steal his physical body, transforming him into a silhouette. Shadow must defeat the demon bosses and seal the Gates, establishing the presence of Shadow Energy in the physical world.

Era II: The Shades Crisis

Shadow Rifts (Shades)

Directly following the sealing of the Gates, mysterious Shadow Rifts tear open across the lands. Shadow, still adapting to his human form, battles mutating shadow remnants, balancing the elemental shadow blooms before they corrupt the fabric of reality.

Era III: The Faction Division

The Great Convergence (SF3)

Decades pass. Shadow Energy has permeated society, splitting humanity into three distinct ideological paths. The Legion builds a wall to quarantine it; the Dynasty lives in harmony with it; and the Heralds analyze it scientifically. Shadow's descendants decide the timeline convergence.

Era IV: The Temporal Clash

The Shadow Mind's Tournament (SF4: Arena)

The Shadow Mind, a sentient force of pure Shadow Energy, tears open space-time. It gathers the greatest champions, commanders, and assassins from all eras and conflicting timelines into the Arena, forcing them to fight in a perpetual tournament for supreme energy control.

FACTION MATCHUP MATRIX

Each faction's primary mechanic excels against one style but falters against another. Explore the rock-paper-scissors dynamic:

🛡️ LEGION VS DYNASTY

Advantage: Legion's **Unbreakable State** absorbs fast, multi-hit Dynasty strikes, allowing them to trade blows and smash through combos.

Weakness: Slow weapon recovery makes Legion easy targets for Dynasty's acrobatic dodges and close-quarters throws.

🐉 DYNASTY VS HERALDS

Advantage: Dynasty's **High mobility & speed** can close gaps, interrupting the Heralds' slower, precise setups before they can land critical hits.

Weakness: Relying on continuous hit combos makes them vulnerable to the Heralds' instantaneous counter-attacks.

👁️ HERALDS VS LEGION

Advantage: Heralds' **High-reach critical slashes** and instant counters can strike Legion warriors just before or after their unbreakable windows open, knocking them down.

Weakness: Low defense armor rating means Heralds will take fatal damage if they fail to interrupt a Legionnaire's heavy strike.
